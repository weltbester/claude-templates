# DOCUMENT ANALYSIS

## Instruction styles
+------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------+
|Instruction Style                                           |Claude’s Behavior                                           |
+------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------+
|Broad, unspecific request                                   |Produces generic summaries or high level descrioptions      |
+------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------+
|Focused, purpose-driven request                             |Targets relevant sections and provides stronger insights    |
+------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------+
|Step-by-step workflow                                       |Builds depth, consistency, and accuracy across artifacts    |
+------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------+
|Context about audience                                      |Adjusts tone, detail level, and structure                   |
+------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------+
|Highlighted sections or priorities                          |Directs Claude’s attention to the most important content    |
+------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------+

## How to Work with Long Documents and Artifacts

- Artifacts provide a stable workspace for summaries, comparisons, and revisions.
- Claude can maintain context across long documents when tasks are well-defined.
- Clear goals improve analysis quality and help Claude focus on what matters most
