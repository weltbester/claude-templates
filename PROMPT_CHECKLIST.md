# Prompt Checklist

1. What's the goal?

2. Do we need Claude to take on a role?

3. What context does Claude need?

4. Who is the audience?

5. What constraints or format do we need?

6. Do we need examples?

7. What tone or style should the AI use?

8. Are there vague words we should replace?

9. Does the prompt clearly express exactly what we want?
